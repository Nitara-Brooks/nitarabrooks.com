About:


Nitara is an artist and current graphic design senior at Appalachian State University based in Durham, North Carolina.

She works in a multitude of mediums ranging from digital to fiber arts.
Her practice is deeply based around human connection whether that be inner, with others, or with nature. She strives to make people more understanding to create more empathetic environments and safe spaces.

In her free time she enjoys gardening, reading, singing/songwriting, playing instruments, hiking, and discovering local artists and businesses.
