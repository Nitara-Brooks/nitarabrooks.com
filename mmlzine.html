Music as a Means of Living

2025



This is a supporting piece to my poster series “Music as a Means of Living.” Due to the posters’ original size, they are only accessible in a gallery space. By compiling them into a zine, they became portable, manageable, and easier to share. “Music as a Means of Living is an exploration of experiences and goals I look forward to in life. Music is a powerful tool and has helped me work through feeling/ expressing emotions. It is not the theme of this project but the catalyst for an extensive line of thinking. I’ve created five posters to help embody my findings based on the themes of Music, Love, Hate, Discovery, and Independence.”
