Bivvy

2025



An imagined branding and call to action for a website, envisioned by Amalie Mccracken, that helps people find activities to do around their local areas. For this specific project, we worked on the branch for Boone, North Carolina.

Bivvy believes in the excitement and spontaneity of life! New experiences are always just around the corner, especially in your local community! With our amazingly curated quiz, you can find a random adventure to spice up your daily routine!




The client wanted an outdoor feel similar to the nostalgia of summer camp, while not pigeonholing the brand too much. Although the activities center around the outdoors, it is not the only type of activity the website provides access to.





I ran with the summer camp aesthetic and developed a brighter color palette, expressing excitement and the activeness of Bivvy. When it came to typography, I gave a slight nod toward vintage camp and hiking guides, a notion mentioned by the client in our meeting.

The logo itself is active in the way the v and y jump to interlock with other letters. That combined with the iv ligature responds to the way the resource connects and creates community as individuals using Bivvy explore their local area.




The call to action has a playful format, portraying inclusivity when it comes to who can use the website. I wanted to avoid seeming too high brow or monetarily inaccessible. Bivvy's vision allows for activities that vary in price range meaning anyone can be satisfied with the service.
