Black American Gothic

2025



Inspired by a fascination for the Southern Gothic, this poetry book, “Black American Gothic: The Critique of a Country Built on Hate,” came to life. It embodies the struggle black Americans have faced for centuries. When we think of Gothic literature, we picture monsters, mutilation, and horrors beyond belief. It was never a metaphor for the black populous but a reality. The books leads readers to understand what goes on, why, and what they can do to help.
