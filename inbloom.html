In Bloom

2024



Branding for the spring 2024 Graphic Design BFA Senior Exhibition at Appalachian State University. With this collaborative brand identity, we aimed to create a sense of childlike joy and growth as these designers moved from the comfort of college to the “real world.” Assigning a system of flower icons, each artist and project was easily identifiable.

I contributed to early ideation and production, printing vinyl, stickers, and supplementary material.
